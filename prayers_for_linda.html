May God Be With You

Linda Mouzon

Linda is currently at Evergreen Rehab and nursing home in Rome Ga.

She there due to a stroke that caused her to have dementia. She is doing well and happy most days with little to no pain

She is still a God fearing woman and knows that Jesus is king of kings.

The family ask that you keep her in your prayers because this has been a tough process for her and sometimes she does have moments where she is in the present and she will say that she doesn't know what is wrong with her and she feels like she is losing her mind
